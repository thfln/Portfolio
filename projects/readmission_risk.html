🏥 Readmission Risk Predictor

This project predicts 30-day hospital readmission risk using clinical and demographic features, supporting proactive interventions, and improved patient outcomes.

📈 Data Sources

  • Hospital Readmission Prediction(synthetic-dataset)

⚙️ Models

  • Random Forest
  • XGBoost

📊 Key Visuals

.

🔍 Key Findings

  • Random Forest achieved the highest recall for high-risk patients.
  • Feature importance revealed that discharge destination was a key predictor for both models while XGBoost was capable of pulling out nuances such as comorbidities and demographic features.
